Course Structure

The course structure has 5 modules; choosing the 1st three modules along with the Differential Calculus could prove sufficient enough to crack CSIR-NET-JRF exam in Mathematical Sciences. The modules being offered are:
  • Group Theory
  • Linear Algebra
  • Real Analysis and Sequences and Series of Real Numbers
  • Differential and Integral Calculus
  • Differential Equations and Vector Calculus

Course Features

  • 3 - live online classes per week where we will discuss the concepts from a topic in-depth, length and breadth. Problems from the previous year question papers related to the topic will be solved.
  • Provision of notes that will be useful for revision and recollection of the concepts discussed, after each class.
  • A recorded version of the live classes will be available in case you are unable to attend the live sessions.
  • Availability of the mentor for any doubts or concept clarification at any given point of time.
  • You being an active member of this Math family and influencing the group with your intellectual thoughts will result in the success of each other.

Course Structure

The course structure has 5 modules. The modules being offered are:
  • Group Theory
  • Linear Algebra
  • Sequence and series of real numbers
  • Functions of single variable and Functions of several variables
  • Differential equations and Integral calculus

Course Features

  • 4 - live online classes per week where we will discuss the concepts from a topic in-depth, length and breadth. Problems from the previous year question papers related to the topic will be solved.
  • Provision of notes that will be useful for revision and recollection of the concepts discussed, after each class.
  • Availability of the mentor for any doubts or concept clarification at any given point of time.
  • After each topic, you will be given challenging assignments that will help you gauge your understanding level and enable you to improve on them.
  • You being an active member of this Math family and influencing the group with your intellectual thoughts will result in the success of each other.
